About Me

Hello, my name is Soe Pyae Sone (aka Nway Oo Pyae Zo). I am a Front-End Developer and Data Analyst with a passion for building responsive, user-friendly websites and transforming data into actionable insights.

I specialize in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, Power BI, and Excel, and I am also skilled in Bootstrap, basic Python, and Git/GitHub for version control.

I have worked on projects ranging from personal portfolio websites to interactive dashboards, which helped me practice both coding and data visualization. I enjoy taking complex information and presenting it in a clear, engaging way.

My strengths include problem-solving, attention to detail, adaptability, and teamwork. I am eager to apply my skills in a professional environment where I can continue learning, contribute to impactful projects, and grow into a more versatile developer and analyst.
